This is OneWorld.

OneWorld is a multi-accessible platform that offers many digital facilities for both the visually and hearing impaired. The OneWorld Project offers direct virtual support to users and allows them to experience their everyday activities with greater independence and no persisting challenges. The goal of our project is to unify people towards one initiative; accessibility for all.
